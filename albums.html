What the Critics Say

Following his previous three-track EP, GetHammerd's “Bangerz Only” is the hallmark of a skilful musician doing what he does best – creating a sound that you can’t pigeonhole. It’s a spellbinder full of rumbling tribal percussion, twinkling melodies and ambient textures all executed with finesse – standing defiantly in the face of musical conformity.
